## Content Production Tips

### Carousel Posts
- **Tool:** Canva (free templates)
- **Size:** 1080x1350px (4:5 ratio)
- **Fonts:** Bold, easy to read
- **Colors:** Green (#10b981), white, dark gray
- **Max slides:** 10 (but 5-7 is ideal)

### Reels
- **Tool:** Reels editor or CapCut
- **Size:** 1080x1920px (9:16 ratio)
- **Length:** 7-30 seconds (short = better retention)
- **Captions:** Always add text overlays (sound off viewing)

### Single Images
- **Tool:** Canva
- **Size:** 1080x1350px (4:5 ratio)
- **Keep it simple:** One message per image

### Stories
- **Tool:** Instagram Stories editor
- **Size:** 1080x1920px (9:16 ratio)
- **Interactive:** Use polls, questions, quizzes
