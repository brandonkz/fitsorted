FAQ

How does it work? +

Just send a WhatsApp message with what you ate — like "2 eggs on toast" or "Nando's quarter chicken". Our AI agent breaks it down instantly with calories, protein, carbs, fat, and fibre.

Do I need to download an app? +

No. It works entirely in WhatsApp. No app download, no account creation, no passwords. Just message our number and start tracking.

How accurate is it? +

We use a curated database of 530+ South African foods with verified nutritional data — including Nando's, Steers, KFC, Woolworths, biltong, vetkoek, bunny chow, and more. For foods not in our database, our AI estimates based on standard portion sizes.

How much does it cost? +

FitSorted starts with a 7-day free trial with all features unlocked. After that, it's R36/month or R399/year (just R33/mo — save 8%). That's just over R1 a day. You won't be charged anything during your trial. Payment is handled securely through PayFast — South Africa's trusted payment gateway.

How do I subscribe / pay? +

After your free trial, simply type "subscribe" or "upgrade" in the WhatsApp chat. You'll receive a secure PayFast payment link. We accept credit/debit cards, instant EFT, and SnapScan. Your subscription starts immediately after payment.

How do I cancel? +

Easy — just type "cancel" in the WhatsApp chat anytime. Your subscription will be cancelled immediately. No contracts, no lock-in, no cancellation fees. You can also email us at hello@fitsorted.co.za if you prefer.

What about restaurant food? +

We have data for Nando's, Steers, KFC, McDonald's, Kauai, Spur, Ocean Basket, Debonairs, Woolworths ready meals, and more. Just type the restaurant name and what you ordered.

Is my data private? +

Yes. We never share your food logs or personal data with anyone. See our Privacy Policy for full details.
