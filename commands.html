📋 Commands & How to Use FitSorted

Everything you can do with the WhatsApp bot

🍽️ Log Food

Just type what you ate
Natural language — no special format needed
Example: "2 eggs, toast, coffee"
📸 Send a photo
Take a pic of your meal — FitSorted will identify it and log the calories
Works for restaurant meals, home cooking, packaged foods
yesterday: [food]
Log something you ate yesterday
Example: "yesterday: chicken curry"
undo
Remove your last food entry
delete [food name]
Remove a specific food from today's log
Example: "delete banana"

📊 Check Your Day

log
See everything you ate today + calories + macros
"how am I doing?"
Quick check — calories vs goal, where you're at
"can I eat [food]?"
Ask if a food fits your remaining calories PREMIUM
Example: "can I eat a burger?"

⚖️ Track Weight

weight [number]
Log your weight in kg
Example: "weight 82.5"
weight history
See your last 7 weigh-ins + trend
projection
Based on your weight trend, where will you be in 30/60/90 days?

🏃 Log Exercise

Just describe your workout
FitSorted tracks calories burned
Example: "ran 5km" or "45 min gym"
exercise log
See today's workouts

🍺 Track Drinks

Log drinks like food
Type what you drank — calories are tracked
Example: "2 beers" or "gin and tonic"
drinks
See drunk-o-meter — how drunk are you? Can you drive?

🧠 Ask Questions PREMIUM

Ask anything
Meal ideas, nutrition advice, food swaps, macro help — personalised answers
Example: "what should I eat for lunch?"
Example: "is oats better than cereal?"
suggest
Get meal ideas based on your remaining calories

💰 Track Food Spending PREMIUM

R[number] or [food] R[number]
Log the cost of your meal
Example: "R45" or "burger R65"
budget
See how much you've spent on food today vs your daily budget

📱 Account & Settings

status
Check your subscription (Free/Trial/Premium), trial days left, renewal date
upgrade
Get Premium — R49/mo with 7-day free trial
goal [number]
Change your daily calorie goal
Example: "goal 2000"
profile
See your stats (weight, height, age, activity level, goal)
reset
Start over with a fresh setup
export
Get your full food log as a CSV file
help
Quick command reference
