📋 Commands & How to Use FitSorted

Everything you can do with the WhatsApp bot

🍽️ Log Food

Just type what you ate
Natural language — no special format needed. Log multiple items at once.
Example: "2 eggs, toast, coffee with milk"
📸 Send a photo
Take a pic of your meal — FitSorted will identify it and log the calories
Works for restaurant meals, home cooking, packaged foods
yesterday: [food]
Log something you ate yesterday (or any past day)
Example: "yesterday: chicken curry" or "monday: braai"
undo
Remove your last food entry. Also works: "oops", "take that back", "delete last"
correct
Got wrong calories? Say "correct" or "wrong calories" to manually fix it
delete [food name]
Remove a specific food from today's log
Example: "delete banana" or "remove coffee"

🍽️ Custom Foods

save [name] = [calories]
Save a custom food with its calorie count for quick logging
Example: "save mom's bobotie = 450"
my foods
View all your saved custom foods
Also works: "saved foods", "food list", "my list"

📊 Check Your Day

log
See everything you ate today + calories + macros
Also works: "today", "summary", "what have I eaten", "day so far"
yesterday summary
View a past day's full food log
Also works: "summary monday", "log 3 days ago"
"how am I doing?"
Quick check — calories vs goal, where you're at
"can I eat [food]?"
Ask if a food fits your remaining calories PREMIUM
Example: "can I eat a burger?"

⚖️ Track Weight

weight [number]
Log your weight in kg (or just type a number like "82.5")
Example: "weight 82.5" or just "82.5"
weight history
See your last 7 weigh-ins + trend
Also works: "my weight", "weight trend", "all weights"
projection
Based on your weight trend, where will you be in 30/60/90 days?
Also works: "what if", "weight projection", "should weigh"

🏃 Log Exercise

Just describe your workout
FitSorted estimates calories burned and adds them to your daily budget
Example: "ran 5km", "45 min gym", "30 pushups", "walked to the shops"

🍺 Track Drinks

Log drinks like food
Type what you drank — calories and alcohol units are tracked
Example: "2 beers", "gin and tonic", "glass of wine"
drinks
Drunk-o-meter — how drunk are you? Can you drive? Estimated BAC.
Also works: "alcohol", "drunk", "bac"

🧠 Ask Questions PREMIUM

Ask anything
Meal ideas, nutrition advice, food swaps, macro help — personalised to your goals
Example: "what should I eat for lunch?"
Example: "is oats better than cereal?"
Example: "high protein snacks under 200 cal?"
suggest
Get meal ideas based on your remaining calories for the day

💰 Track Food Spending PREMIUM

R[number] or [food] R[number]
Log the cost of your meal alongside the calories
Example: "R45" or "burger R65"
budget
See how much you've spent on food today vs your daily budget

🎁 Invite Friends

invite
Get your personal referral link. Earn R10 off for every friend who joins!
Also works: "referral", "share", "refer"
promo [code]
Redeem a promo or coupon code
Example: "promo FITSA50" or "code FRIEND10"

📱 Account & Settings

status
Check your subscription (Free/Trial/Premium), trial days left, renewal date
Also works: "subscription", "my plan"
upgrade
Get Premium — R49/mo with 7-day free trial
Also works: "pro"
reset
Recalculate your goals — update weight, height, age, activity level
export
Get your full food log as a CSV file (emailed to you)
export week
Get a weekly summary report emailed to you
support
Something not working? Report a bug or get help
Also works: "stuck", "not working", "broken", "help me"
delete my account
Delete all your data permanently (POPIA compliant)
help
Quick command reference
Also works: "menu", "commands", "features"
