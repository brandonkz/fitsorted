Kgosientsho Ramokgopa Under Fire: Power Strategy Delays!

Energy Minister Kgosientsho Ramokgopa is in hot water as South Africa waits for a new power strategy. Apparently, there are disagreements with Spain that are holding everything up. Because, you know, why would we want a smooth energy plan when we can just bicker with other countries?

This delay is just another nail in the coffin for a country already struggling with loadshedding. As if we didn’t have enough problems, now we have to deal with the fact that our government can’t even agree on a plan to fix our power issues. It’s like watching a group of toddlers fight over a toy—frustrating and utterly pointless.

Why does this matter? Well, every day without a solid power strategy means more blackouts and more frustration for South Africans. Businesses are suffering, and families are left in the dark—literally. We need action, not more delays!

So, what’s next? Hopefully, Ramokgopa can sort things out with Spain and get this power strategy moving. But until then, we’re all just left wondering when the lights will stay on for good.

In the meantime, let’s just keep our fingers crossed and our candles handy. Because it looks like we’re in for a long wait!
