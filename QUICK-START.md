## 🎨 Quick Brand Reference

### Colors
- **Primary:** Green `#10b981` (buttons, highlights, accents)
- **Text:** Dark gray `#1a1a1a` (body text)
- **Background:** White `#ffffff` (clean, simple)

### Fonts
- **Primary:** Inter (or Helvetica/Arial if Inter not available)
- **Style:** Clean, modern, sans-serif

### Voice
- Conversational, like a helpful mate
- South African slang OK ("howzit", "lekker", "boet")
- Encouraging, not preachy
- Practical, not perfectionist

**Example tone:**
✅ "Howzit! Log your breakfast and let's see where you're at."  
❌ "Please input your morning meal consumption for nutritional analysis."

## 💡 Pro Tips

### TikTok
- **Hook in 2 seconds or they scroll** (first frame must stop them)
- **Text overlays are mandatory** (40% watch with sound off)
- **Trending sounds boost reach** (check TikTok trends weekly)
- **Post at 7 AM, 12 PM, or 7 PM SAST** (highest engagement)

### Instagram
- **Reels > Feed posts** (Reels get 2-3x more reach)
- **Carousels > Single images** (people swipe, boosts engagement)
- **Stories daily** (keeps you top-of-mind)
- **Reply to comments in first 2 hours** (boosts algorithm)

### Both Platforms
- **Consistency > perfection** (post 5 average videos > 1 perfect video per month)
- **Engage with your niche** (like/comment on 20 SA fitness accounts daily)
- **Test everything** (try different hooks, styles, formats)
- **Batch content** (film 10 videos in one session, edit/post throughout week)

---

## 🚨 Common Mistakes to Avoid

❌ **Posting randomly** (be consistent, 5-7x per week minimum)  
❌ **Ignoring comments** (reply to EVERY comment, especially in first 2 hours)  
❌ **Not using trending sounds** (TikTok favors trending audio)  
❌ **Weak hooks** (first 2 seconds decide if they watch or scroll)  
❌ **No CTA** (always tell them what to do: "WhatsApp +27690684940")  
❌ **Giving up after 2 weeks** (growth takes 30-90 days)
