How to eat smarter at Woolies

TIP 1
Check the "per serving" — some meals are 2 servings
That 350 cal meal? Could actually be 700.
TIP 2
Stir fry > anything with cream sauce
Alfredo has 2x the calories of the stir fry.
TIP 3
Add your own veg to stretch portions
Microwave broccoli + Woolies meal = filling dinner under 400 cal.
