## Next Up (v2)

### Estimated Meal Pricing (NEW - unique differentiator)
- Show approximate rand cost alongside calorie counts for restaurant meals
- "Quarter chicken + spicy rice + chips ~ R109 | 848 cal | R0.13/cal"
- Cost-per-calorie metric (nobody else does this)
- Rough estimates only (~R109), clearly labelled as approximate
- Major SA chains first: Nando's, Steers, KFC, Kauai, Woolworths, McDonald's
- Content unlocks: "cheapest way to hit protein goal", "R50 vs R150 lunch calories", "healthy week under R500"
- Update prices quarterly (or when users flag changes)
- Display as optional line: "~R109" next to calorie output

### Photo-Based Food Recognition
- Snap a picture of your plate, AI identifies and logs the meal
- Reduces friction even further (no typing needed)

### Weekly Progress Reports
- Weight trends, average intake, consistency scoring
- Sent via WhatsApp every Sunday evening

### Influencer/Coach Referral Programme
- Fitness coaches and creators earn 20% recurring commission
- Referral tracking via unique WhatsApp links

### Expanded SA Food Database
- Continuous additions based on user requests
- Chicken Licken, Spur, Ocean Basket, Wimpy, Roman's Pizza
- Homemade SA recipes (bobotie, potjie, melktert)
