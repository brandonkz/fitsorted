# FitSorted Image Prompt Template

Create a clean, realistic food photo suitable for a health/fitness brand.

Requirements:
- natural lighting
- appetizing, balanced meal
- minimal props, clean background
- no text, no watermark, no logos
- no camera UI, no HUD, no overlays
- no fake “4K” labels
- avoid obvious AI artifacts

Style:
- modern, premium, Instagram-ready
- shallow depth of field
- warm tones

Output:
- 1024x1024 or 1080x1080
- single subject meal (no collage)
