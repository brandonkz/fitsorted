FitSorted for Business
Nutrition Tracking for Corporate Meal Programs

The Problem

Your clients invest in quality meals for their teams. But employees have no easy way to track what they're eating, hit their nutrition goals, or see how your meal plans support their health.

That's a missed opportunity — for their wellness, and for your business.

FitSorted is a WhatsApp-based nutrition tracker built for South Africa. We partner with meal providers to give your corporate clients a branded, effortless calorie and macro tracking experience — powered by your actual menu.

For Employees

Pick from today's menu with exact nutrition data — no guessing. Or type/photograph any meal. Get daily check-ins and track progress over time.

For Corporate Clients (HR / Wellness)

Weekly nutrition reports per team. Wellness KPIs: average calories, macro balance, participation rates. Zero setup — employees click a link to join.

For You (The Meal Provider)

Unique referral codes per corporate client. Monthly dashboard with signups, active users, and most popular meals. A premium value-add that differentiates you from competitors.
