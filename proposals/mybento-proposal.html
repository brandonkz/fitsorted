📱 How It Works

💬
It's just WhatsApp. Employees text what they ate — or snap a photo — and get instant calorie and macro tracking. Morning check-ins, evening summaries, coaching mode, weight tracking. No app download. No login. No friction.

Employee Experience

Employee clicks a join link (unique per company). Texts the bot like a friend: "two eggs on toast" or sends a photo. Gets instant tracking with SA-specific food data.

Corporate Integration

Each MyBento client gets a unique referral code. Monthly reports show active users, average calories, participation rates. Data feeds into MyBento's wellness dashboard.
