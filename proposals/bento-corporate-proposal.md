# FitSorted for Business
### Nutrition Tracking for Corporate Meal Programs

---

## The Problem

Your clients invest in quality meals for their teams. But employees have no easy way to track what they're eating, hit their nutrition goals, or see how your meal plans support their health.

That's a missed opportunity — for their wellness, and for your business.

## The Solution

**FitSorted** is a WhatsApp-based nutrition tracker built for South Africa. We partner with meal providers like you to give your corporate clients a branded, effortless calorie and macro tracking experience — powered by your actual menu.

No app downloads. No sign-ups. Just WhatsApp.

---

## How It Works

### For Employees
1. **Text the bot** — tell it what you ate (or snap a photo)
2. **Instant tracking** — calories, protein, carbs, fat logged in seconds
3. **Daily check-ins** — morning recap and evening summary via WhatsApp
4. **Your menu, pre-loaded** — employees can pick from today's menu with exact nutrition data (no guessing)

### For Corporate Clients
- Weekly nutrition reports per team (PDF to HR)
- Wellness KPIs: average calories, macro balance, participation rates
- Zero setup — employees just click a link to join

### For You (The Meal Provider)
- Unique referral codes per corporate client (`JOIN-YOURCO-ABSA`, `JOIN-YOURCO-FNB`)
- Monthly dashboard: signups, active users, most popular meals
- A premium value-add that differentiates you from competitors
- Data on which meals are most popular (product insights)
