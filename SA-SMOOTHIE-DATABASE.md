## Usage in FitSorted

Users can log with any of these formats:

**Short:**
- `peanut butter bomb`
- `strawberry sunrise`
- `green machine`

**With size:**
- `peanut butter bomb large`
- `strawberry sunrise regular`

**With brand:**
- `nu peanut butter bomb`
- `kauai strawberry sunrise`

**Colloquial:**
- `pb bomb` (Nu)
- `pb power` (Kauai)
- `choc shake` (Kauai)
