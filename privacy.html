FitSorted Privacy Policy

Plain English summary of how we handle your data under POPIA.

Who we are

FitSorted is operated by AlphaX Asset Capital (Pty) Ltd.

What data we collect

We only collect what we need to provide the service.

  • Phone number
  • Weight, height, age, and sex
  • Food logs
  • Workout logs
  • Water intake
  • GLP-1 medication data

Why we collect it

We use this data to calculate your nutrition targets and to provide tracking, summaries, and reminders.

How we store your data

Your data is stored in a Supabase cloud database. Supabase servers are located outside South Africa.

Third parties we use

  • Meta and WhatsApp for messaging delivery
  • OpenAI for food parsing and nutrition estimation

Data retention

We keep your data while your account is active. You can request deletion at any time.

Your rights under POPIA

You can ask for access to your data, request corrections, or ask us to delete your data.

How to delete your data

Send the message "delete my data" to the FitSorted bot and follow the confirmation steps.

Contact

If you have questions, email us at info@fitsorted.co.za.
