FitSorted Privacy Policy

Plain English summary of how we handle your data under POPIA.

Who we are

FitSorted is operated by AlphaX Asset Capital (Pty) Ltd.

What data we collect

We only collect what we need to provide the service.

  • Phone number
  • Weight, height, age, and sex
  • Food logs
  • Workout logs
  • Water intake
  • GLP-1 medication data

Why we collect it

We use this data to calculate your nutrition targets and to provide tracking, summaries, and reminders.

How we store your data

Your data is stored securely on servers in South Africa. We use encrypted backups and restrict access to authorized personnel only.

Third parties we use

  • Meta/WhatsApp — Message delivery (you control this by using WhatsApp)
  • OpenAI — Food parsing and calorie estimation (no data stored by OpenAI)
  • PayFast — Payment processing when you subscribe (they handle card data, not us)

We do NOT sell your data

Your food logs, weight, and personal information are never sold to third parties. Ever.

Data retention

We keep your data while your account is active. You can request deletion at any time. Deleted data is permanently removed within 48 hours.

Your rights under POPIA

South Africa's Protection of Personal Information Act (POPIA) gives you the right to:

  • Access — Request a copy of your data (type "export" in the bot)
  • Correction — Update incorrect information (type "reset" to update your profile)
  • Deletion — Permanently delete your account and all data (type "delete" in the bot)
  • Objection — Stop processing your data (delete your account)

How to export your data

WhatsApp the bot and send: export

You'll receive a JSON file with all your data (profile, food logs, exercise logs).

How to delete your data

WhatsApp the bot and send: delete

Confirm deletion and your account will be permanently removed within 48 hours.
