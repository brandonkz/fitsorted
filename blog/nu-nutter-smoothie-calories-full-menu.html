How to Actually Stay on Track at Nu

Here's the thing: Nu isn't bad. The food is decent quality, and if you're trying to gain weight or fuel heavy training, those 800-calorie smoothies might actually be useful. But most people walk in thinking they're making a healthy choice and walk out having consumed half their daily calories without realizing it.

Here's how to not get wrecked:

  • Stick to Regular sizes. The jump from Regular to Large adds 200-300 calories. You don't need it.
  • Avoid anything with peanut butter. It's calorie-dense as hell. 2 tablespoons = 190 calories, and Nu doesn't skimp.
  • Order bowls over smoothies. Solid food fills you up better than liquid. An Açai Bowl will keep you satisfied longer than a smoothie with the same calories.
